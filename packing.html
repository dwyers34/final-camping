Packing

What to pack when camping

Disclaimer: This packing list is only a suggestion of what to bring and all of this is not required to have an enjoyable camping trip. Items can be added to the trip if desired as well depending on the weather.

Around the Campsite

  • Tent
  • Sleeping Bag
  • Pillows
  • Swiss Army Knife
  • Flashlight
  • Extra Batteries
  • Backpack
  • Reusable Water Bottles
  • Sunscreen

Clothing and Footwear

(Dependent on Weather)
  • T-shirts
  • Undergarments
  • Sunglasses
  • Hat
  • Jacket(with hood)
  • Boots/Sneakers
  • Shorts
  • Long Pants
  • Long-Sleeve Shirts
  • Sleepwear

Food

  • Granola Bars
  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Bagels
  • Fruit(dried or fresh)
  • Raw Vegetables
  • Smores Essentials

Personal Items

  • Toilet Paper
  • Insect Repellent
  • First-Aid Kit
  • Toothbrush/Toothpaste
  • Biodegradeable Soap
  • Hand Sanitizer
  • Shower Water Bag

Other Items

  • Binoculars
  • Camera
  • Maps
  • Sketchpad with Art Supplies
  • Radio/Music Player
  • Travel Alarm Clock
  • Umbrella
